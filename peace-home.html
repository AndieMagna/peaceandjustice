Goal 16: Peace, Justice and Strong Institutions

Promote peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provide access to justice for all and build effective, accountable and inclusive institutions at all levels.

What is SDG Goal 16?

Without such societies, sustainable development cannot be achieved. Goal 16 for Sustainable
Development promotes peaceful and inclusive societies for sustainable development, provides
access to justice for everyone at all levels, and promotes strong, accountable institutions at all
levels. These all targetissues of reducing violence, human rights protection and an enhanced rule
of law, enabling people for a life free from fear and from injustice. Raising awareness on
governance corruption, enabling accountability, and empowering community members lead to a
world-forming ideal based on inculturation-affected fairness, safety, and trust among every society.

Here are the main targets that the United Nations set to achieve by 2030:

1. Reduce violence and deaths — Significantly reduce all forms of violence and related death rates everywhere.

2. Stop Abuse and Exploitation — Stop abuse, exploitation, trafficking and all forms of violence against children.

3. Justice for All — Make sure that everyone has equal access to justice.

4. orruption and Bribery Reduction — Substantially reduce corruption and bribery in all their forms.

5. Successful Institutions — Build effective, transparent, accountable, and inclusive institutions at every level.

6. Participatory Decision Making — Ensure that decision-making in all institutions is responsive and inclusive.

7. Protect First Rights and Fundamental Liberties — Guarantee the right to access information and protect fundamental rights in accordance with national law and international commitments.

8. Strengthen About-the-law Institutions at the National Level — Support countries in preventing violence and fighting terrorism, and promoting peace.

9. Promote Non-discriminatory Laws and Policies: Laws and policies should ensure equal treatment and inclusion for all.

Key Focus Areas

Peace

Reduce violence and promote safety in communities.

Protect vulnerable groups from abuse and exploitation.

Encourage dialogue and peaceful conflict resolution.

Justice

Ensure equal access to justice for all.

Strengthen the rule of law and accountability.

Eliminate corruption and misuse of power.

Institutions

Build transparent and accountable governments.

Improve institutional efficiency and fairness.

Uphold and protect human rights for everyone.

Inclusivity

Promote participation in decision-making.

Enforce equality through fair laws and policies.

Empower citizens to have a voice in governance.
